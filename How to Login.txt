How to Login:

  1. Open your browser to: http://localhost:5000
  2. To login, visit: http://localhost:5000/api/dev/login
    - This will automatically log you in as the dev superuser
    - You'll be redirected back to the homepage, now logged in
  3. To logout: http://localhost:5000/api/logout
